UNITREE G1 · MUJOCO · RL

G1 Humanoid — Kung Fu Motion Learning

Trained reinforcement learning policies for the Unitree G1 humanoid in MuJoCo to replicate kung fu motion sequences. The agent learns to mimic martial-arts-inspired moves purely from reward signals, producing physically plausible and visually expressive motion — a foundation for sim-to-real transfer of agile whole-body behaviours.

Go2 Quadruped — Autonomous Exploration & Mapping

The Go2 quadruped autonomously explores and maps an entire building or room without any human intervention — navigating unknown spaces, building a full map on the fly, and detecting obstacles in real time.
